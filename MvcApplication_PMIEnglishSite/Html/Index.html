Introduction to Pioneer Materials Inc.

Pioneer Materials Inc. (PMI), was incorporated in the state of Delaware in 2000, and has operations in the state of California, USA. In 2008, the company launched a wholly owned subsidiary in Chengdu, China for its primary manufacturing facility. PMI was founded by veterans from the semiconductor, magnetic recording, and sputtering taget industries focusing on high performance materials applications. The primary products of PMI are materials for semiconductor memory chips, thin film solar PV, thermo-electric for clean energy harvesting and other advanced coating. The products come in the form of sputtering targets, various chalcogenide as well as metallic compounds and powders of various sizes and purity. The company is ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified, and has a portfolio of intellectual properties related to the relevant materials. PMI serves customers in key markets in North America, Europe, and Asia. 


The wholly owned subsisiary of PMI in China is housed in a  41,000 square foot facility, and has capability of various compound formation, pulverization, and sintering processing. 

Company Mission

To be a world-class supplier of the innovative materials in clean energy harvesting,  semiconductor and other advanced materials applications.
