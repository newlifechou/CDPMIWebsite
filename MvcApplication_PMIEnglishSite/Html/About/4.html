PMI History

  • In Yr. 2000, PMI was registered in the state of Delaware in United States 

  • In Yr. 2008, formed a US wholly owned foreign entity in Chengdu, China. The company has built 41,000 sq.ft factory space in Chengdu, China, with an annual capacity of 14,400 targets 

  • In Yr. 2008, the company's Chengdu subsidiary was ISO 9100 and ISO14001 fully certified

  • In Yr. 2008, the company has successfully developed an array of high-tech materials, including a fully selenized CIGS target for thin-film PV application, and semiconductor materials BiSeTe, BiSbTe for thermo-electric energy harvesting application. 

  • In Yr. 2009, the company formed a strategic partnership with thin-film photovoltaic equipment suppliers and provided vertically integrated turn-key solutions. For example, such a strategic partnership was formed with Swedish thin-film PV equipment provider Midsummer AB. 

  • In Yr. 2011, the company successfully developed variety of semiconductors made of chalcogenide compounds and glasses for the next generation non-volatile memory applications. Formed critical strategic relationship with a few world class memory chip manufacturers and research institutions. 

  • In Yr. 2013, the company's innovative materials were integrated successfully in the prototype of next generation non-volatile memory chips.
